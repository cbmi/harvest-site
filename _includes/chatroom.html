Do you have any questions about Harvest? Hop into the Harvest chatroom to talk to members of the Harvest team. The chatroom is powered by Slack. If you have an @email.chop.edu, @cchmc.org, @mtu.edu, @ohsu.edu, @seattlechildrens.org or @childrens.harvard.edu email address, you will be able to create a Slack account and join the chatroom. Otherwise, request an invitation to join the chatroom by sending email to info@harvest.io.
